“Do I emit CO2 when I surf the internet?”, “Your internet habits are not as clean as you might think” or “What's the carbon footprint of the world wide web?”

Heard these before? Our online activity has consequences for the environment. Currently web accounts represent 2% of all greenhouse gas emissions, which is equivalent to the global aviation industry or the emissions of Germany, the sixth most polluting country in the world.

Our mission is not to web shame your online presence, but instead to create awareness. Our Web Carbon Calculator analyzes your website and suggests how to optimize your website to become more sustainable. We want to motivate you towards having a more sustainable online presence.
